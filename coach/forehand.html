Forehand

Basic Topspin Volley Slice Lob
Preparation
Get into ready position
Semi-western / full western / eastern grip
Non-dominant hand on throat for stability.
Stance
Start with your feet shoulder-width apart
Turn your body sideways to the net
Bend your knees slightly and get into a low, athletic stance
Keep your non-hitting hand on the racket for balance
Stay on the balls of your feet for quick movement
Step forward with your front foot as you swing the racket
Backswing
Start with a relaxed grip on the racket
Turn your shoulder for backswing
Bring the racket back with a smooth and controlled motion, keeping it at or above shoulder height
Rotate your hips and shoulders as you bring the racket back
Aim to have the racket face perpendicular to the ground at the top of your backswing
Keep your eyes on the ball throughout the backswing
Maintain a consistent and smooth backswing rhythm
Forward swing or Contact point
Eyes on the ball
Contact point in front of the body
Arm fully extended at contact
Wrist firm and slightly angled
Face your racket in the direction you want the ball to travel.
Wrist snap for topspin/backspin
Smooth acceleration through contact
Follow through
Keep your arm relaxed
Rotate your body and finish the swing over your shoulder
