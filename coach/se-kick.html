Serve

Basic Flat Slice Kick
Preparation
Feet firmly on the ground
Turn your body sideways to the net
Ideally, using a continental grip
Head, eyes will look over your shoulder at your target.
Stance
Platform-Wide or pinpoint stance
Start with your feet shoulder-width apart
Bend your knees and get into a balanced stance
Toss the ball up in front of you with your non-hitting hand
Bring your racket back behind you in preparation for the serve
Step forward with your front foot and explode up to hit the ball
Backswing
Hold the racket with a firm grip and bring it back with a controlled motion
Keep your toss arm extended and your hitting hand relaxed
Keep the racket head up and make L formation with your hitting arm
Keep your eyes on the ball throughout the serve motion
Maintain a smooth and consistent rhythmic motion backswing tempo
Contact point
Eyes on the ball
Contact point in front of the body
Toss the ball at the side of the body
Aim to hit hte ball above and slightly fot topspin
Arm fully extended at contact
Racket head pointing towards the target
Follow through
Finish by snaping your wrist and racket across your body.
