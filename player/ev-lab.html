Forehand

Basic Topspin Volley Slice Lob
Preparation
Anticipate the lob and move quickly to the ball's path
Hold the racket with a continental grip (similar to the handshake grip) or eastern grip or whatever feels comfortable to you.
Stance
Stand sideways with your feet shoulder-width apart
Bend your knees slightly and position your body weight on your back foot
Backswing
Use a long backswing to generate power and height on the lob
Take the racket back above your shoulder and keep your hitting arm extended
Forward swing or Contact point
Make contact with the ball slightly in front of your body and above your shoulder
Aim to contact the ball with the strings of the racket
Keep the racket face open at contact
Follow through
Follow through with your hitting arm extending upwards towards the direction of the lob
Finish the stroke with your racket head pointing towards the sky
